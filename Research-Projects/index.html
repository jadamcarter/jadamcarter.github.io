Research Projects

  1. ERC Advanced Grant: KNOW-HOW (2025-2030)
    I am PI on a 5-year European Research Council Advanced Grant KNOW-HOW: A new framework for theoretical and practical control across intelligence domains (EUR 2,480,625), which will run from October 2025, and which will include a large team.
  2. UKRI Navigating the Neural Frontier: Embedding Epistemology and Ethics into Neurotechnology (2026-2028)
    I am co-PI (along with Simon Hanslmayr) on a 2-year research grant (~£1,000,000). The other Co-Is are: Emma Gordon, Mona Simion, Chris Kelp, Daniele Faccio, Monika Harvey, Lars Muckli. The project runs from April 2026 - April 2028 and includes four postdocs.
  3. AHRC Digital Knowledge (2022-2026)
    I am PI on a 3-year research grant Digital Knowledge: A New Framework for Digital Epistemic Virtues (the Co-I is Jesper Kallestrup, Aberdeen) (£980,602.20). The project runs from September 2022 - May 2026 and includes two postdocs and a PhD student.
  4. AHRC Expanding Autonomy (2022-2026)
    I am Co-I on a 4-year research grant "Expanding Autonomy: Scaffolded, Embedded and Distributed" (£974,052.08) (the PI is Neil Levy, Oxford). The project runs from September 2022 - September 2026 and will include two postdocs.
  5. Leverhulme A Virtue Epistemology of Trust (2019-2024)
    I was PI on a 3-year research grant A Virtue Epistemology of Trust (Co-Is: Chris Kelp and Mona Simion) from the Leverhulme Trust (£254,871). This project ran from 2020-2023 and included a PhD student and Postdoc.
  6. British Academy Small Grant (2019)
    PI (co-I: Chris Kelp): British Academy Small Grant (£10,000).
